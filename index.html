I am a computer science student @ The University of Birmingham in my final year. My primary interests lie in cryptography, especially the intersection of real-world and theoretical cryptography. I also have a latent interest in proof assistants and type theory, and would love to understand more category theory.

I am currently looking at key correlated security of message authentication codes, with a look at both providing formal definitions and proofs, as well as bridging the gap to concrete constructions wherever possible. For more on KCA security see here. I hope to upload my project on this topic here once it has been reviewed.
