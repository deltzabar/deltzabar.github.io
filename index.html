Call me Sof :) I am a soon-to-be PhD student in Canada (location to be confirmed...). My primary interests lie in cryptography, especially the intersection of real-world and theoretical cryptography. I care about the impacts of cryptography on its end-users, and to this end I am interested in settings such as private informational retrieval & anonymous communication. Additionally, I have a latent interest in proof assistants and machine-verified cryptography particularly in how they can increase trust in privacy guarantees.

My final year project looked at key correlated security of message authentication codes. It provided formal definitions & proofs, while bridging the gap to concrete constructions wherever possible. For more on KCA security see here. Will link my project in a sec.
